Our Essence

Beyond the Bean

Founded in 2024, Keofi was born from a singular obsession: to strip away the noise and focus on the purity of the roast. We don't just serve coffee; we provide a moment of stillness in a chaotic world.

Every batch is roasted in micro-lots, ensuring that the unique profile of the altitude and soil remains intact. From the mountains of Ethiopia to your cup.

Precision

Measured to the milligram. Brewed to the second. Excellence is non-negotiable.

Direct Trade

We work directly with farmers, ensuring they earn 40% above market rates.

Minimalism

Removing the unnecessary to highlight the extraordinary character of coffee.
